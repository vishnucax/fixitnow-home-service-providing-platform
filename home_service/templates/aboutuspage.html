Our Story

FixItNow was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make home repairs and maintenance hassle-free for homeowners while providing skilled professionals with a platform to grow their businesses.

What started as a small local service has grown into a nationwide platform serving over 50,000 happy customers across the country. We're proud to have created a community where trust and quality service come first.
